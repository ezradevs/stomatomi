Atomi is rebranding to Stomatomi

We’re evolving our identity with a clean, welcoming look inspired by the biology of learning. New name, same mission: clear teaching that helps every student thrive.

By Stomatomi Editorial Team · August 27, 2025

Why “Stomatomi”?

In plants, stomata are tiny pores that open and close to regulate gas exchange — a simple, elegant system that keeps life in balance. We think learning works the same way: open to ideas, exchange knowledge, grow with confidence.

Stomatomi is a nod to clarity and growth. It’s still us — just fresher and more focused on what matters.

Same mission

Clear, engaging lessons — unchanged.

Fresh identity

Blue‑and‑white palette, crisp typography, calm visuals.

Seamless transition

Your classes and logins continue to work as usual.

FAQ

Is my Atomi account still valid?

Yes. Your credentials and classes continue to work — no action required.

Do links or videos change?

Existing links will redirect. New content will carry Stomatomi branding.

Why a biology‑inspired name?

It captures our ethos: openness to ideas and visible growth, like stomata enabling life.
